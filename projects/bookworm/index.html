Bookworm

Category: Software
Project Website: https://github.com/Bookworm-project

Bookworm makes it easy to interactively explore massive collections of texts as data. If you have a huge collection of texts, it provides a way to interpret them, make them explorable, and unlock them to a wide variety of uses even if you can't share them freely for copyright or other reasons.

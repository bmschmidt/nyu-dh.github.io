City Record Project

Digitized copies of the New York City Record from 1873 - 1949.

Category: Externally Funded
Project Website: http://cityrecord.engineering.nyu.edu/

The City Record Project hosts the New York City Record from its first volume in 1873 through 1949. The City Record contains data on every aspect of the cityâ€™s politics, society, economy, real estate and infrastructure development, employment, and expenditures and aids scholars studying the city because of the depth and breadth of the data it contains, offering digitized resources unmatched by any other city. Supported by a NEH Humanities Collections and Reference Resources grant, the digitized and searchable PDF documents openly accessible to the public.

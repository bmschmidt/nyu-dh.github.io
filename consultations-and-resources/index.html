Consultations and Resources

There are lots of people and services at NYU to help with digital humanities work from fresh ideas to established projects. The best way to find and identify resources is to ask for a consultation with one of the support groups.

  • Digital Scholarship Services (request appointment) for help with digital publishing & web hosting, project design & scoping.
  • Data Services (request appointment) for help with data management & analysis, or if you have digital materials you want help with preservation & archiving
  • Email dh.help@nyu.edu for help with project design & scoping, with questions about seed grants, curricula, or other granting programs.
